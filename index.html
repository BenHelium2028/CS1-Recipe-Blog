Description

Made with ripe bananas and served over vanilla ice cream. Serving for one person, scale ingrideince as nececary. It was a personal favoite of my grandfater's and it my favorite dish to cook on this websight.

Ingredients

  • 1 Banana sliced in peices
  • 2 Table Spoons Butter
  • 3 Table Spoons Brown Sugar
  • 1 Table Spoon Honey
  • A Dash of Dark Cinemon
  • 1/2 Small Lemon's Worth of Juice
  • 1/2 Small Orange's Worth of Juice
  • 1/2 Ounce Rum
  • ! Ounce Banana Liquor

Steps

  1. Melt butter, brown sugar, honey and citimen togeather.
  2. Add lemon and orange juice conservitivly when fully mixed.
  3. Add bananas to the mixture and cook until they are soft and not mushy. (pro tip: Use slighly unripe bananas to make them cook slower)
  4. Add banana liquor.
  5. Add rum and quickly set in on fire onced added to flambe while moving the pan.

    6. Serve over Vennilla Ice Cream

  • 1 parsnip
  • 1 small turnip
  • 1 large union cut into quaters
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 3 stems fresh parsley
  • 12 cups of water
  • 4 buillon packets

Steps

  1. place the chicken in boans into a large pot fallowed by the water
  2. bring to a boil than slightly lower heat
  3. cook about 2 hours or when the chicken is tender
  4. remove chicken or carrot and set aside
  5. discard wings, beef bones, clery, onions, parsnip, turnip, and parcley
  6. wait for the soup to cool, in the meantime dice the chicken and carrots
